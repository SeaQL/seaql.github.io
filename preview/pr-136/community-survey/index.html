Community Survey


SeaQL is an independent open-source organization. Our goal is to empower engineers with comprehensive and flexible tools to develop applications in Rust. To achieve this goal, we need to understand you, the developer, better.


By completing this survey, you will help us gather insights into how developers are using our libraries and identify means to improve the developer experience. We have limited resources, as such we have to prioritize our attention to maximize the impact of our work.


We will also publish an annual survey report to summarize our findings.
